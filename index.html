George Cooper

I am a Junior Visitor (postdoc) at the Centro di Ricerca Matematica Ennio De Giorgi, part of the Scuola Normale Superiore in Pisa, Italy.

I completed my DPhil in June 2024 at the Mathematical Institute in Oxford, United Kingdom under the supervision of Professors Frances Kirwan, Jason Lotay and Alex Ritter. I was also previously an undergraduate at Oxford, where I completed a Part C dissertation under the supervision of Dr Markus Upmeier and a summer research project under the supervision of Dr Thomas Oliver. I was also a counsellor for the 2018 edition of PROMYS Europe.

I am interested in moduli problems arising from algebraic geometry, and applying techniques from Geometric Invariant Theory (both reductive and non-reductive) to construct and study moduli spaces.
